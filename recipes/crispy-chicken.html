Cispy fried chicken

KFC like chicken is the best chicken you will ever need. Why? Because it combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

  • chicken

    Cut a flour-pound whole chicken into pieces or purchase flour pounds of breasts, drumsticks, wings, legs, and/or thighs at the grocery store.

  • Buttermilk

    Acidic buttermilk tenderizes the chicken without makng it too tough. Also, it helps the flour mixture stick to the chicken.

  • Flour

    All-purpose flour gives the buttermilk and seasonings something to stick to, while ensuring a wonderful crispy crust.

  • Seasonings

    This crispy fried chicken recipe calls for paprika (which helps with browning), salt, and pepper. You can add more spices and seasonings to taste.

  • Oil

    Vegetable oil is perfect for frying chicken because it has high smoke point.

Steps

  1. Make the Breading

    Combine the flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in a zip-top-bag. Seal the bag and shake until well-combined.

  2. Dredge the Chicken

    Pour buttermilk into a shall bowl, then dip the chicken until it's completely soaked in buttermilk. Place the soaked chicken in the bag with flour mixture, seal, and shake well to coat. Transfer the coated chicken to a baking sheet, and cover. Allow the flour coating to become a paste-like consistency.

  3. Fry the Chicken

    Pour the oil in a skillet and bring it to high heat. Brown the chicken on both sides, then reduce the heat, cover the skllet, and cook for about 30 minutes. Remove the cover, return the heat to high, and fry until extra crispy. Drain the chicken on paper towels.
